How it works

From swipe to shoot
in four steps

Snupix makes the process of finding and working with creative collaborators as simple as possible.

1
Create your profile
Set your role, upload your portfolio, and tell the community what kind of shoots you're looking for.
2
Swipe & match
Browse curated profiles. Swipe right on creatives whose work excites you. It's a match when both of you are interested.
3
Plan your shoot
Chat, share moodboards, confirm the date and location, and get reminders leading up to shoot day.
4
Share & grow
Post your best shots to the community board, collect reviews, and watch your creative network expand.

Safety First

Built for trust from
day one

Every feature is designed with the safety of models and photographers in mind.

🪪
ID Verification
Optional government ID upload for a verified badge. Builds trust without being mandatory for everyone.
🔞
18+ Confirmation
Models must confirm they are 18 or older before their profile goes live. No exceptions.
🚨
Report System
Models can report photographers for inappropriate behavior. Reports are reviewed and action is taken.
📊
Cancellation Tracking
Cancellations are marked on profiles so the community can see who is reliable and who isn't.
📍
Transparent Shoot Details
All shoot events require location, date, wardrobe, and MUA info upfront — no surprises on the day.
🤝
Mutual Confirmation
Both parties confirm 3 days before the shoot and can request changes or cancel with full transparency.
